Meet Milestone

Standard to-do lists fall apart when you're trying to achieve something complex—like learning Python, reading a 500-page book, or prepping for a massive interview. They just aren't built for deep, structured learning.

That's why we built Milestone.

Milestone lets you build a beautiful, hierarchical curriculum. It tracks your progress at every level, supports rich markdown notes, and best of all: it can be completely automated using AI.

Why not just use Notion or Trello?

Built for Hierarchy

Most task apps are flat lists or kanban boards. Milestone enforces a strict 3-tier system (Category → Module → Step). This naturally maps to courses, books, and study guides without getting messy.

Granular Progress

Instead of just "To Do" and "Done", Milestone gives you exact percentage readouts at the Module, Category, and Global levels. You always know exactly how close you are to the finish line.

Lightning Fast & Offline

Because it uses your browser's local storage, there is zero loading time. Click a checkbox, and the UI updates instantly. You can use it on an airplane or train without Wi-Fi.

AI-Ready Imports

Milestone was designed with a generic JSON parser. You can ask an AI to generate a 50-step course, paste the code into Milestone, and it builds the entire UI instantly. No manual typing required.

How it Works (The 3 Tiers)

1

Workbooks

The overarching container for your goal. Example: "Full Stack Development Bootcampt".

2

Categories

Visual sections inside your workbook to keep things tidy. Example: "Frontend", "Backend", "Databases".

3

Modules & Steps

The actual actionable items. Example: "Module: React Hooks", "Step 1: Learn useState".

Make it Yours: Custom Terminology

Not every project uses "Modules" and "Steps". When you create a Workbook, you can rename the core components so the entire app speaks your language.

Coding Course
Modules → Labs
Steps → Checkpoints
Reading a Book
Modules → Chapters
Steps → Pages
Fitness Goal
Modules → Weeks
Steps → Workouts
Project Prep
Modules → Milestones
Steps → Tasks

Bonus: You also pick a custom Theme Color and Icon for every workbook, completely changing the UI's vibe when you open it.

Rich Text & Markdown

A step title like "Install Dependencies" isn't always enough. Sometimes you need to remember how to install them.

Milestone supports full Markdown inside the Description of every step. Just click the chevron arrow next to a step to reveal your rich notes, complete with inline code blocks, bold text, nested lists, and hyperlinks to external documentation or YouTube tutorials.

Visualizing Progress

Nothing feels better than watching a progress bar fill up. Milestone tracks your completion automatically at three levels:

The Micro Level (Modules)

The sidebar updates your module percentages in real-time. Hit 100%, and it turns into a satisfying green checkmark.

The Macro Level (Categories)

Back out to the Workbook dashboard to see beautiful progress bars for each category. Great for seeing if you are falling behind in a specific area.

The Ultimate Goal (Workbook)

Pinned to the top of your screen is the master progress bar. Watch it glide toward 100% as you conquer your goals.

Share via Link (Read-Only)

Built a masterpiece curriculum and want to show it off? You can now instantly share your workbooks with the world using a simple link.

Generate a Link

Click the Share icon on any workbook card. Milestone will generate a unique URL that you can copy and send to friends, classmates, or post online.

Safe & Read-Only

When someone opens your shared link, they enter a Read-Only Mode. They can explore your modules, read your markdown notes, and interact with the UI to see how it works, but they cannot alter or delete your original data.

The AI Magic Trick

Don't want to type out a 50-step curriculum by hand? Let AI do the heavy lifting. Milestone has a built-in JSON importer that lets you paste generated courses directly from ChatGPT, Claude, or Gemini in seconds.

Try this prompt right now:

I want to master [Insert Any Topic]. Create a comprehensive learning roadmap for me with 20+ distinct steps, organized into logical categories from Beginner to Advanced.

Output the result strictly as a raw JSON array matching this exact schema format (do not include markdown formatting or extra text outside the JSON):

[Click "Import" in Milestone to get the Sample JSON, and paste it here]

Paste the AI's response into the Milestone Import box and watch your screen populate with a full course instantly.

Creative Playbook

Because you can change the terminology and use markdown, Milestone is a chameleon. Here are some of our favorite ways to hack the system:

Q&A Flashcards

Prepping for an interview? Put the question in the Step Title, and hide the answer in the Markdown description. Expand the step to reveal the answer, and check it off if you get it right!

Question (Title): What is a Closure?
Answer (Desc): [Hidden until you click the arrow...]

RPG Quest Tracker

Gamify your life. Set your Module Term to "Quest" and Item Term to "Objective". Give your workbook a cool color and a sword icon.

Category: Real World Chores
Quest: Clean the Kitchen
Objectives: Slay the dishes, empty the trash.

Language Vocab Builder

Group words by category (e.g., "Food", "Travel"). Use the Step Title for the foreign word, and the description for the translation and a practice sentence.

Module: Dining Out
Word: La Cuenta
Notes: "The bill". Usage: La cuenta, por favor.

Daily Routine Tracker

Create Categories for "Weeks". Create Modules for "Days" (Monday, Tuesday). Set your steps to habits: Drink Water, Meditate, Code for 1 Hour.

Offline, Sharing & Privacy

Milestone is designed to be fully owned by you. It operates 100% in your browser. There is no cloud database watching you, which unlocks some incredible benefits:

Works Offline

Take your laptop on an airplane or the subway. Milestone requires zero internet connection to read, edit, or check off your progress.

Share with Friends

Because workbooks are just JSON data, you can build a masterpiece curriculum, export it, and email the file to a classmate or friend to import!

Important Warning: Because your data lives in your browser cache, clearing your history or browser data might wipe your workbooks!

Always use the Settings menu in the top right of the main app to regularly Export All Data as a backup file to keep your progress safe.
